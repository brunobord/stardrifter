Take your World Sheet, your Ship Sheet and your Character Sheet and check if you want to buy or sell goods. You'll be able to buy Merchandises (things to trade from a World to another), sell these Merchandise, or buy things to improve your ship performances.

On the World Sheet, you see the price of available goods. On the Ship Sheet, you can see how many cargo load unit are available. On the Character Sheet, you can see how much credit you have and what is your Negociate score.

Buy Merchandise

Every time you want to buy things, you first pick the item and the quantity you want to buy. Then you roll 2D6+Negociate. See the result below:

Roll Result
6 or less The item you want to buy is 25% more expensive
7-9 The item you want to buy is available at its normal price
10 or more The item you want to buy is 25% less expensive
